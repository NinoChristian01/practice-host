In the dance of life, where many paths unfold, There's one companion,
loyal and bold. With fur as soft as the morning's light, My dog, my
friend, my constant delight. His eyes, like pools of warm, brown earth,
Reflect a love of boundless worth. A wagging tail that speaks volumes
true, Of joy and comfort found in you. He greets each day with eager play
, A bounding spirit that chases away, The shadows of life, the stress, the
strife, Replacing them with laughter, with zest for life. In quiet
moments, by my side he lies, A gentle presence, a comforting sigh. His
steady breath, a lullaby so sweet, In his company, my heart finds its
beat. Through rain and storm, through sun and snow, He walks beside me,
wherever I go. A faithful guide, a steadfast friend, On whom I know I can
depend. He teaches me of love so pure, Of happiness in simple lore. To
find delight in little things, In chasing dreams and butterfly wings. So
here's to my dog, my friend so true, For every moment shared with you. In
your love, I find my home, My heart, my joy, in you alone.
